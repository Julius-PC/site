About

I am a Junior at New York University studying Recorded Music at the Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music, and Double Majoring in Computer Science at the College of Arts & Sciences. I was born in Hong Kong and went to international school there. At the age of 13, I moved to England for boarding school for 5 years of my life, until coming to America for university.

Interests

Music

Growing up, I learned to play piano. I had always been passionate in singing, so combining the two practices, my interest in music skyrocketed. I took on the guitar, saxophone, and music production alongside my passions. In my spare time, I release music under the moniker CHRIS KENG.

Technology

I had always been tech savvy growing up, playing around with electronics and eventually developed a passion for computers. Alongside school, me and a peer have founded a startup called 'melodi', a project management software designed for independent musicians, where I have led the technical production of the software
