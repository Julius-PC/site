About MORE力

Live Entertainment • Artist Management • Hong Kong

Our Mission

MORE力 aims to celebrate Hong Kong culture and reignite its status as an entertainment powerhouse by highlighting the creativity and talent shaped by growing up in the 852— “We give people a reason to return.”

Our Vision

The character 力 means “strength” or “force.” MORE力 suggests a push for more energy, more courage, and more presence for Hong Kong voices in the regional and global music scenes.

MORE力 will become a global entertainment enterprise that champions the Hong Kong diaspora, emphasizing the fusion of culture through music.
